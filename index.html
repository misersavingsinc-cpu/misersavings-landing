Common questions

A few things
people always ask.

What does Miser cost?
Free to download. A service fee of $2.50 + 1.5% of each transfer (no cap) applies when money moves — the full fee schedule is shown before anything is confirmed. Withdrawals are always free.
Where does my money actually go?
Into your own savings account — not ours. Miser can't access, spend, or keep your money. Every 7 days, on your personal cycle day (set by your first save), your saved total moves from your linked checking to your savings via ACH, run by our banking partner.
What happens if I miss a day?
Your streak counter resets to zero. Your rank does not. Once you've earned a rank, the crest stays — even if you start a new streak from scratch. Progress is permanent; streaks are just motivation.
How do I get my money back out?
Your savings live in your own savings account at your own bank — so you get money out the way you always have: through your bank, any time. Miser never pulls money out of your savings, and there are no penalties, fees, or waiting periods.
