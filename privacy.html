Privacy Policy

Effective: April 19, 2026 · Last updated: April 19, 2026

MiserSavings Inc. ("mi$er," "we," "us," or "our") builds a mobile savings app that helps you move small, skipped purchases onto a virtual prepaid debit card (your vault). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and the choices you have. By using mi$er, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information we collect

We collect only the information we need to operate mi$er, keep your money safe, and comply with the law. We break it into four categories.

Information you give us

  • Account info: email address, name, date of birth, phone number, and password credentials (handled by our authentication provider, Clerk).
  • Identity information (KYC): legal name, date of birth, residential address, Social Security Number (last 4 or full, as required), and a photo of a government-issued ID. This is required by federal law to open a financial account.
  • Goals and skips: the goals you create, the skips you log, amounts, categories, and notes.
  • Support messages: anything you write to us at support@misersavings.com or through the in-app support channel.

Information from your bank

  • Bank account details via Stripe Financial Connections: when you link a bank account to fund your vault, Stripe Financial Connections provides us the account and routing numbers, account owner name, balance, and (with your consent) transaction history used for ACH verification and fraud prevention. We do not store your bank login credentials — those stay with Stripe.
  • Transaction data: the deposits, transfers, and withdrawals that happen inside your mi$er vault.

Information collected automatically

  • Device & app info: device model, operating system version, app version, language, time zone, and IP address.
  • Usage data: screens you visit, buttons you tap, and features you use — used to improve the product and diagnose issues.
  • Crash and diagnostic logs: automatic reports when the app crashes or encounters errors.

Information from third parties

  • Identity verification: our card program partners and their vendors may check the identity information you provide against credit bureaus, public records, and fraud databases to verify who you are. This is required to comply with the USA PATRIOT Act's Customer Identification Program (CIP) and Bank Secrecy Act (BSA).

2. How we use information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and maintain your account and your vault.
  • Verify your identity and comply with anti-money-laundering (AML), Know-Your-Customer (KYC), OFAC sanctions, and other financial regulations.
  • Move money between your linked bank and your vault via ACH.
  • Calculate and credit card program fees and balances.
  • Charge the $3.49 withdrawal fee at the point of withdrawal.
  • Send you required communications (transaction confirmations, fee disclosures, Regulation E notices, tax forms, and legal notices).
  • Send you product updates, tips, and milestone notifications (you can opt out of marketing messages at any time; you cannot opt out of legally-required communications).
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraud, unauthorized access, and abuse.
  • Improve, debug, and secure the mi$er app and our services.
  • Respond to lawful requests from courts, regulators, and law enforcement.

4. MiserCard & our card program partner

mi$er is a financial technology company, not a bank. Your vault balance is held on a virtual prepaid debit card (the MiserCard) issued through our card program partner. mi$er does not hold your funds directly.

The MiserCard is a prepaid debit card, not a bank deposit account. It is not insured by the FDIC in the same manner as a traditional savings or checking account. No interest or APY is paid on your MiserCard balance. The purpose of the MiserCard is behavioral — to help you separate saved money from your day-to-day spending account.

Your money is always yours. There are no lock-up periods. You can withdraw your full balance to your linked bank account at any time for a $3.49 flat fee. Always keep your login, email, and phone secure to protect your account.

5. Bank linking (Stripe Financial Connections)

When you link a bank account to mi$er, we use Stripe Financial Connections to do it. Here is what that means in plain language:

  • You sign into your bank through Stripe's secure flow. mi$er never sees your bank username or password.
  • Stripe returns a permission token and the account details needed to run ACH transfers (account and routing numbers, account owner name).
  • You can review and revoke the linked-bank permission at any time from within mi$er or directly at Stripe's permission center.

6. Security

We take security seriously because money is involved. Our safeguards include:

  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • All data at rest in our databases is encrypted using AES-256.
  • Passwords are never stored in plaintext; Clerk hashes them using bcrypt.
  • Sensitive identity information (SSN, ID images) is held by Stripe, our financial partner — not in our own database.
  • Row-level security on our Supabase database ensures users can only read and write their own data.
  • Administrative access to our systems requires multi-factor authentication and is logged.
  • We run automated monitoring for suspicious activity and unauthorized access attempts.

No system is perfectly secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security. If you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately at support@misersavings.com.

7. Data retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is open, plus the longer of:

  • Five (5) years after your account is closed, as required by the Bank Secrecy Act and our card program partners; or
  • Seven (7) years for tax-related records, as required by the IRS; or
  • Any longer period required by applicable law, regulation, or a legal hold.

After retention periods expire, we delete or anonymize the data.

8. Your rights & choices

You have the following rights with respect to your personal information, subject to applicable law and our regulatory obligations:

  • Access: request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: ask us to fix inaccurate information.
  • Deletion: ask us to delete your personal information. We may retain information we are legally required to keep (see Section 7).
  • Portability: request a machine-readable copy of information you provided us.
  • Opt out of marketing: unsubscribe from marketing emails using the link in any email, or change notification preferences in the app. You cannot opt out of legally-required transactional communications.
  • Close your account: at any time, from the Profile tab or by emailing support. Your vault balance will be withdrawn to your linked bank account (subject to the $3.49 fee) before the account is closed.

To exercise any of these rights, email support@misersavings.com. We will respond within 30 days (or 45 days if we need an extension and notify you of the delay).

9. California residents (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the rights described in Section 8, plus the following under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) as amended by the California Privacy Rights Act (CPRA):

  • The right to know what categories of personal information we collect, the categories of sources, the business purposes for collection, and the categories of third parties we share it with (described throughout this policy).
  • The right to limit our use of "sensitive personal information" (such as your SSN). We only use sensitive personal information for purposes permitted under CCPA — principally, to comply with KYC/AML law.
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising these rights.

We do not sell or "share" personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising as those terms are defined under CCPA/CPRA.

10. Children's privacy

mi$er is not intended for and is not directed at children under 18. You must be 18 years of age or older to open a mi$er account. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a child has provided us information, email us at support@misersavings.com and we will delete it.

11. International users

mi$er is available only to residents of the United States. Our services, servers, and card program partners are located in the United States, and by using mi$er you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in the United States. The United States may not provide the same level of data protection as your country of residence.

12. Changes to this policy
