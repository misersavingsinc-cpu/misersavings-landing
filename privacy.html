Privacy Policy

Effective: April 22, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 26, 2026

Pre-launch notice. MiserSavings is currently in development. This policy covers our waitlist and website only. A full privacy policy covering financial data will be provided before any money movement features are enabled.

1. Who we are

MiserSavings Inc. is a Delaware C-Corp. Questions: support@misersavings.com

2. What we collect

Currently we only collect:

  • Email address — when you join the waitlist.
  • Basic usage data — pages visited, browser type, IP address, via standard server logs.
  • Aggregated analytics — anonymized traffic and engagement data collected through Google Analytics (page views, traffic source, device type, country). IP addresses are anonymized before being processed and stored.

We do not collect financial information, Social Security Numbers, bank account details, or payment data at this time. When financial features launch, a separate detailed privacy notice will be presented before you can use them.

3. How we use it

  • To send you waitlist updates and early-access invitations (unsubscribe anytime).
  • To operate and improve the website.
  • To comply with applicable law.

4. Who we share it with

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with:

  • Email service providers — to send waitlist communications.
  • Hosting providers — to operate the website.
  • Analytics providers — Google Analytics processes anonymized usage data on our behalf to help us understand how the website is used. Google's privacy policy applies to that processing.
  • Law enforcement — if required by law.

5. Data retention

We retain your email address until you unsubscribe or request deletion. You can request deletion at any time by emailing support@misersavings.com.

6. Your rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold about you. Email support@misersavings.com.

7. Cookies

This website uses two categories of cookies:

  • Essential cookies — required for basic site functionality.
  • Analytics cookies — set by Google Analytics to measure aggregated usage (page views, traffic sources, device type). These do not personally identify you. You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on or by enabling Do Not Track / Global Privacy Control in your browser.

We do not use advertising or behavioral targeting cookies.

8. Children

Our services are not directed to anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Changes

We may update this policy at any time. We will post the updated date at the top of this page. Material changes to financial data handling will require explicit notice before any affected features are used.
