5. Your accounts, deposits & withdrawals

How it works

mi$er connects to two of your own bank accounts via Stripe Financial Connections: your linked checking account (the source) and your linked savings account (the destination). Both accounts are yours — held at your bank, in your name. mi$er never receives or holds your funds. Money movement between your own accounts is initiated by our ACH processing partner (disclosed in-app at the time you authorize a transfer).

Deposits (skip batches & Add Money)

When you log a skip, mi$er records a commitment to save that amount. Every Sunday, your week’s skips are batched into a single ACH transfer from your linked checking account directly to your linked savings account. You may also add money at any time via the “+ Add Money” feature, which triggers an immediate ACH transfer between your same two accounts.

By using the app you pre-authorize mi$er to initiate these ACH debits from your checking account on your behalf. We will not overdraw your account.

If a transfer fails (for example, insufficient funds), we will notify you in-app and may pause skip-logging until resolved.

Withdrawals

Because your savings are already in your own savings account at your bank, your money is always directly accessible through your bank. Withdrawals are free. There are no lock-up periods — your money is always yours.

Unclaimed property

If your account becomes dormant under applicable state unclaimed property law, we will use reasonable efforts to contact you before any action is taken.
