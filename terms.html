5. Your vault, deposits & withdrawals

You fund your vault by logging "skips" in the app. Each logged skip triggers an ACH transfer from your linked external bank account into your vault. You authorize mi$er (and our banking partner) to initiate each such ACH debit for the amount you specified. Linked bank account access is provided by Stripe Financial Connections.

If a deposit fails (for example, insufficient funds or a bank-side block), we may retry, reverse the skip, and/or suspend skip-logging until the issue is resolved. We do not overdraw your external bank account on your behalf.

Withdrawals

You may withdraw any amount up to your full vault balance at any time, subject to our banking partner's processing limits. Withdrawals are delivered to your linked external bank account via standard ACH, which typically settles in 1–3 business days.

There are no lock-up periods. Your money is always yours.
